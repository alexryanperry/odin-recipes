Pancakes

Description

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Water, egg, or milk
  • Baking powder
  • Chocolate chips (not optional)

Steps

  1. Add together flour, liquid, chocolate chips, and baking powder (don't make the mixture too wet or dry, find the sweet spot!).
  2. Heat pan over medium-high heat and add a lot of butter.
  3. Add the batter to the pan and cook until the top starts to bubble.
  4. Enjoy!
