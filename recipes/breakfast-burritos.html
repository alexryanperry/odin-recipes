Breakfast Burritos

Description

These burritos of breakfast will explore the region beyond your anus as you moan in abject agony from the porcelain throne.

Ingredients

  • Tortillas
  • Bacon, sausage, or chorizo (extra pain)
  • Eggs
  • Cheese
  • Potatoes
  • Salsa of your choice
  • Seasoning of choice (salt and pepper + plus whatever you feel like)

Steps

  1. Cook the potatoes until they are about halfway done (you can almost stick a fork through them.
  2. Add the meat to the potatoes until both are nearly cooked and then add the eggs.
  3. Season to taste.
  4. Take the mixture off of heat and warm the tortillas.
  5. Place the meat/potatoes on the tortillas and add the cheese/salsa.
  6. Wrap and get ready for eruption.
