Tacos

Description

This classic taco recipe from the depths of west Ventura will be sure to channel your inner cholo.

Ingredients

  • Corn tortillas
  • Your meat of choice that is primarily pre-marinated (pollo, asada, carnitas)
  • Onions
  • Cilantro
  • Salsa of your choice

Steps

  1. Rougly chop the onions and cilantro.
  2. Heat pan over medium-high heat and add oil.
  3. Add oil and once that is sizzling, add the meat and cook through.
  4. Take the meat off of heat and warm the tortillas.
  5. Place the mixture on the tortillas and add the toppings.
